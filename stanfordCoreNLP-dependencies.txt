This application makes use of the following JARs from Stanford Core-NLP :-

ejml-core-0.39.jar
ejml-ddense-0.39.jar
ejml-simple-0.39.jar
stanford-corenlp-4.5.1.jar
stanford-corenlp-4.5.1-models.jar

Please download the stanford coreNLP version 4.5.1 and place the above mentioned JARs in the folder containing SentimentAnalysis.jar
